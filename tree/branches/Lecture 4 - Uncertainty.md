# Key Concepts 
### *Errors*

$$\Delta x = \Delta x_r + \Delta x_s $$
Where $x_r$ are random errors and $\Delta x_s$ are systematic errors.
### *Uncertainty*

Uncertainty $u$ should be treated with an uncertainty of it's own hence it is most often rounded up to two significant digits. For example: we have calculated the the uncertainty of some measurement to be $u = 0,0003241$. It should then be rounded to $u = 0,00033$.
